Meet the cake designer, Suli

Hi, I'm Suli, a cake designer in Atlanta, Georgia. I was born in Guerrero, Mexico and first moved the the United States when I was 8. I started in the restaurant business when I was 17 in order to help my family out, but my passion for cake design really began when I was 13. As the oldest, of five, I wanted to create cherished memories for my younger siblings so on each of their birthdays, I would bake a cake. It started with the boxes you could buy at the convenience store. You know, the ones where you get premixed cakes and have to add an egg? I would buy those and then some candies from the local gas stations to add to them and make them special. It is with these fond memories that I was able to become a pastry chef and cake designer. For 10+ years, I have worked on taking those little boxes of pre-mixed cakes to making cakes from scratch and bringing to life the ideas my customers gave me. My wish is for Dulce Designs to spread ideas and help people find the perfect cake design for them.
